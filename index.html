Sari tithi hindi virama savant me hain 1) Bhima Ram (1914-2004,90yrs) 2) Nayka Ram 3) Jugta Ram a) Samdha Devi 4) Rana Ram 4) Rekhi 5) Badhra Ram 6) Dhudi 7) Fata Ram 8) Kushta Ram 9) Jaisa Ram 1) Bhima Ram (1914-2004,90yrs) Bhima Ram h/o kehni (chohtan ki khitaliyani thi d/o Amra ram jiska ab parivar ni hain ekloti lrki thi ) phli patni thi jinke ek putra nayka ram tha. Baad ki patni Hanju Dhande setrau se thi chahrad ganv ki thi. Unke 2 baad ke putra Jugta Ram and Rana Ram the. Charkha Bunte the. Log baate krte the ki unki hunkar me bhut takat thi ki yha magra se hunkar lgate the to sanau me sunai deti thi, Or apni majbooti ke liye jane jate the. Cows thi or khet jotke khana khate the. Bhima ram aati se 1856 kaal baras me aaye the chohtan bazar me ruke the. Ve yha aaye the to ek bishnoi ladies ne khana khilaya tha jisme khejdi ki saankh mila ke khana khilaya tha. Oonth mar gye the us saal me. Yha Chetan ke ghar ke pass me ek neem hain vha pr log pani na hone se yha se bhag gye the. Koi mjdoori ni thi or na hi kisi ka sahyog tha. Ye log pani ke kua vankalsara basti chohtan se late the. Baad me ye log sasural ke diye khet me aagye magra me. 2) Nayka Ram (1960-2038,68yrs) Nayka ram bolne me bhut hosiyar the. Inki jhalak inke putra badhra ram or phata ram or inki bati dhudi me dikhti thi. Inki beti rekhi ke baare me jyada pta ni chala. Nayka ram dohe (saakh) bola krte the. Bhaipe gya tha nayka ram apni marji se gye the. Mulsingh naam ka bhanej tha. Mulji oonth denge aisi ummid thi. Mulji ne mna kr diya to kharid ke saandh laye the. “Man kre gyo mele moto layo oonth, ghare oone oonth po muho oonth dino toto, aakhi pakhi nange dini ghar no dini poonth jau mulji re ooth jko mulji deve oonth” “100 gau ro pondo kiye gayo khuhdi manjh, theth gye no naah milyo mhare umar ro danjh“ “96ve mo nayko aayo to bai kiya ta thaal , Hanki ghoi nayko aayo nayke me hua haal, Nayko kiyo rhino mulji re kvarda paanch, Nayke kino rhino to mulji re ki aayi oonch” “Oonth to hathe ko aayo handhdi layo Saandhdi hubar n todido lahe, Khetrdo khado phoda pa jahe” 3) Jugta Ram (1975-2023, 48yrs) Jugta ram h/o samdha devi. Bhut hi mehnati the, chohtan ganv me pkka karma bnaya tha majdoori krke jb logo ke kacche makan gobar ke hua krte the. Jugta ram ne uda ram ke ghar ke pass bandh bnaya tha jispr bhut mehnat krte the. Sangram ne btaya ki jugta ram kaafi mehnati insaan the (“khaptiya”). Jugta ram or rana ram ke bhut bnti thi. Jugta ram ki patni Rana ram ki patni ko bhut sunaya krti thi, pr in bhaiyo ki mitrata pr fr bhi km na hue na hi ye log apni ladies ke jhagre pr dhyan dete the. Jugta ram vartman om bnna ke mandir ke samne ki jamin jota krte the. Bhut sari cows thi inki. Ek baar ye cow pilane gye the chipal nadi ke pass ke khet vha inko heart attack hua tha. Pass me kisi ghar me jake inki massage kit hi or inka safa bikhar gya tha. Jab Rana Ram gaadi se khi or kaam pr gye hue the vapis aa rhe the tb dekha ki mere bhai ko aaj kisi ne jhagda krke pita hain kya. Inhone gaadi rukwa ke utre or puchha ki “de khabar bhai thu aaj bikro bikhro lage” Fir inko pta chala ki inki tabiyat shi na hain, fir dr kr pass ganv leke gye. Pr vha inki moke pr death ho gyi. Mere papa ki dadi samdha or akeli ladies or kuchh gents bazar oonth pr gye or vha inhone Jugta Ram ko is halat me dekha. a. Samdha Devi Ye jugta ram ki wife thi. Ye bolne me bhut kadak thi inki bhu or ghar ke pass me inke bolne me dhak thi. Inhone ek bar Rana Ram (sanau Jaisa Ram ke sasur) se bol chal ki. Luni, Rana Ram sanau ki beti jaisa ram ko di hue thi. Jaisa ram key ha oonth the jinki aadat kharab thi. Onth ladies pr door se dood ke aajate the pr lgate ni the. Ye ghatna rana ram sanau ne dekhi to baadror pariwar ko bhala bura bolne lge ki Sali sasu ghar me rhti hain or bhu logo ko bahr bhej diya kaam krne ke liye. Is pr samdha bol pdi or baad me rana ram sanau or samdha ne kabhi milke ram ram na kiya life time.   4) Rana Ram (1982-2058,76yrs) Rana ram dikhne me sundar the. Apni kutniti ke liye jane jate the. Ye acche kpre phanke, or lathi leke ghode pr ghuma krte the, acchi si juti phne hue. By MBaadror
